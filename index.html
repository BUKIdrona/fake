About the Workshop

We are excited to announce QKD Based Fibre Connect 2024, a premier workshop dedicated to exploring the latest advancements and innovations in the field of quantum communication using optical fibre technology. This workshop aims to convene leading experts, researchers, and practitioners to delve into the newest developments and applications in Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) and related quantum communication technologies.



The program will broaden your understanding of hybrid quantum systems, where classical and quantum mechanics integrate seamlessly. This comprehensive agenda is designed to equip participants with a deep understanding of the current challenges and future directions in quantum communications, fostering an environment ripe for innovation and collaboration. Whether you are a seasoned professional or a newcomer to the field, this workshop promises to provide invaluable knowledge and networking opportunities that will drive the advancement of quantum technologies. Join us for a series of insightful talks, technological demonstrations, and networking sessions with leading experts and researchers in the domain.
